Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

In short: Use DailyPDFTask for legitimate purposes, respect copyright and the law, and don't try to misuse the service.

1. Permitted Uses

DailyPDFTask is provided for lawful personal, educational, and professional use. You are permitted to:

  • Convert your own images and documents to PDF
  • Convert documents you have the legal right to reproduce
  • Merge files you own or have permission to combine
  • Edit, compress, and download PDFs you have generated
  • Use the service for personal, academic, or business productivity

2. Prohibited Uses

You may not use DailyPDFTask to:

  • Convert or distribute copyrighted material without authorisation
  • Create PDFs containing illegal content, including but not limited to child sexual abuse material, terrorist content, or incitement to violence
  • Process documents containing malware or malicious code
  • Create fraudulent or forged documents
  • Reproduce documents in violation of confidentiality agreements or court orders
  • Engage in any activity that violates applicable local, national, or international law
  • Attempt to reverse-engineer, decompile, or tamper with the service
  • Use automated bots, scrapers, or scripts to access the service at scale

3. Copyright and Intellectual Property

You are solely responsible for ensuring you have the right to process any file using DailyPDFTask. Do not use this service to infringe the intellectual property rights of others. See our Copyright / DMCA Policy for more information.

4. No Commercial Resale Without Permission

You may not resell, sublicense, or commercially redistribute access to DailyPDFTask or its underlying technology without express written permission from DailyPDFTask.

5. Enforcement

Violations of this Acceptable Use Policy may result in your access to the service being restricted. Since DailyPDFTask is a client-side tool with no account system, enforcement is primarily through legal means for serious violations.

6. Reporting Violations

If you believe someone is using DailyPDFTask in violation of this policy, please contact us.
