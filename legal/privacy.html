🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Short version: DailyPDFTask runs entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. We use trusted services like Google AdSense which may use cookies for ads and performance, but we do not store your documents.

1. Information We Collect

DailyPDFTask is a client-side only application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your files. All PDF conversion and editing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript.

The only data we may store is limited to browser-level preferences:

  • Theme preference (dark/light) — stored in localStorage as pctheme
  • Cookie consent status — stored in localStorage as pc_cookie_consent

These are stored locally on your device only and never transmitted anywhere.

Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: https://adssettings.google.com

2. Files and Documents

Any files you upload to DailyPDFTask are processed entirely within your browser's memory. They are:

  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never stored on disk by DailyPDFTask
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Immediately discarded when you close or refresh the browser tab

You retain full ownership and control of your files at all times.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use localStorage (not cookies) to persist your preferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible by our servers (we have none)
  • Can be cleared at any time via your browser settings

For more information see our Cookie Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The application loads fonts from Google Fonts and JavaScript libraries from CDN providers (unpkg.com, cdnjs.cloudflare.com). These services may collect standard web request data (IP address, browser type) as part of serving those assets. Please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

DailyPDFTask is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data in the traditional sense, most data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) are inherently satisfied. Your only data is in your own browser. You can clear it at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser's localStorage for this site
  • Using your browser's privacy/data settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
