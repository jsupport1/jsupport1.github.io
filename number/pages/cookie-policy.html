Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, analyse traffic, and serve targeted advertising.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function and cannot be disabled.

  • sw-cache — set by our Service Worker for offline functionality. Stores static assets only.
  • dnt_cookie_consent — stores your cookie consent choice in localStorage.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

  • _ga — distinguishes users (2-year expiry)
  • _ga_* — maintains session state (2-year expiry)
  • _gid — distinguishes users (24-hour expiry)

Opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

  • IDE — Google DoubleClick (13-month expiry)
  • __gads — tracks ad frequency

Manage ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.

How to Control Cookies

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Do Not Track

Our site currently does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals.
