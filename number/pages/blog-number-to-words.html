The Ultimate Guide to Number to Words Conversion

Converting numbers into words might seem like a simple elementary school task, but in the adult world of finance, legal contracts, and banking, it plays a crucial role in fraud prevention and clarity.

Why Spell Out Numbers?

When you write a cheque or draft an invoice, numerical digits like "1,200" can easily be altered by a malicious party to "11,200" just by adding a stroke of a pen. However, altering the word "One Thousand Two Hundred" into "Eleven Thousand Two Hundred" requires completely rewriting the text, making fraud immediately obvious.

Indian vs International Systems

Depending on where you live, large numbers are grouped differently:

  • International System: Groups numbers by thousands (Thousands, Millions, Billions). E.g., 1,000,000 is One Million.
  • Indian System: Groups numbers by hundreds after the first thousand (Lakhs, Crores). E.g., 1,00,000 is One Lakh.
