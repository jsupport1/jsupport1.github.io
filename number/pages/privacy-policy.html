Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DailyNumberTask ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit https://dailynumbertask.com. Please read this carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please stop using our site.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

DailyNumberTask is a browser-based tool. All conversions happen on your device. We do not:

  • Collect or store the numbers you enter
  • Require account registration or login
  • Store conversion results on any server
  • Sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties

3. Information Automatically Collected

We may collect non-personally identifiable information automatically:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, OS, referring URL, pages visited, time of visit
  • Device data: Screen size, device type, language settings
  • Usage data: Pages viewed, time on site (via Google Analytics, if enabled)

4. Cookies

We use essential, analytics, and advertising cookies. See our Cookie Policy for full details. You may control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Google AdSense

We display ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve personalised ads. Opt out at adssettings.google.com. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand usage patterns. Data is collected anonymously. Opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

7. Service Workers & Offline Caching

Our Service Worker caches only static public files (HTML, CSS, JS) locally. No personal data is stored in this cache.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us immediately if you believe a child has submitted personal data.

9. Data Security

Since we do not collect your conversion inputs, exposure risk is minimal. The site is served over HTTPS to protect data in transit.

10. Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of linked third-party sites. Please review their policies independently.

11. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You may have the right to access, delete, or port your data, and to opt out of personalised advertising. Contact privacy@dailynumbertask.com to exercise these rights.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Changes are posted here with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@dailynumbertask.com or use our Contact page.
